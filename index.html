Who We Are

Eastburn Trucking & Logistics is a Florida-based company located in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida. The founder, Edwin Rosa, has over 34 years of experience in the trucking industry, bringing skills, knowledge, and expertise to the business. Edwin understands the ins and outs of freight transportation, ensuring that goods are delivered safely and on time. His commitment to quality service has helped the company build a strong reputation for reliability and great customer care.

We work hard to ensure that deliveries are made quickly and efficiently, always putting the needs of the customer first. The company prides itself on its ability to handle urgent shipments and maintain clear communication throughout the delivery process. As Eastburn Trucking continues to grow, it remains focused on delivering outstanding service and fostering strong relationships with clients, making it a trusted choice for businesses in Florida and beyond.
